Backstory of Company

Starting in 2011, C0MMUN1TY was once just a group of passionate gamers and aspiring game developers. Now together, we all have a shared vision of bringing unique and innovative experiences to players.

Why Does the Company Exist?

C0MMUN1TY exists to support everybody who has ever wanted to make a game. So many people have great ideas, but never have the time or money to actually create their dream. This is why we exist.

Our Goals

Our goals are to give anybody that wants to make a game the opportunity to do just that. We want to have hundreds of developers that are constantly striving for new ideas and creating games they are proud of.
